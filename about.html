ABOUT

Welcome to the world of Arable Earth Pvt Ltd. (AEPL), a dynamic startup dedicated to revolutionizing the agricultural sector. Founded by a seasoned team of entrepreneurs with over 30 years of collective expertise in agriculture and related activities, AEPL is driven by a passion for farming and a commitment to realizing Prime Minister Shri Narendra Modi's vision of doubling farmers' income.

Our Vision:

Inspired by the vision of Shri Narendra Modi, AEPL conducted extensive research on the current state of the Indian agricultural sector. Our mission is to introduce innovative farming ideas that significantly increase farmers' income.

Mission:

AEPL's business model focuses on undertaking large-scale commercial and industrial plantations through private partnerships. We are committed to establishing value-adding processing units for end agricultural produce. Our aim is not just to cultivate crops but to create a sustainable ecosystem that benefits farmers and the agricultural industry as a whole.

International Expansion

To explore opportunities both locally and internationally, AEPL is set to open an office in Dubai in 2026. This strategic move aims to tap into agricultural business opportunities in Africa, aligning with our commitment to global agricultural development.

Current Focus

AEPL has identified two primary crops for exploration: Bamboo and Jackfruit. In the initial two years, our focus is on Bamboo. We plan to establish a Biochar unit with a daily production capacity of 120 tons, processing 500 tons of bamboo using state-of-the-art technology. Talks are underway with Indian and European companies for technology transfers to produce Biochar and create organic manures fortified by Biochar.
In the first phase, we propose to establish a bamboo plantation covering 317 acres of land in Chincholi, Gulbarga district, Karnataka. Over the next two years, our ambitious plan is to expand the bamboo plantation to 2,000 acres.
At AEPL, we believe in fostering sustainable agricultural practices and creating a brighter future for farmers. Join us on this exciting journey as we pave the way for a prosperous destination in agriculture.

About Bamboo

Bamboo, a versatile woody grass from the family Poaceae, stands out for its rapid growth, strength, and ecological benefits. With tall, slender culms, it plays a vital role in environmental conservation by quickly regenerating after harvesting. Rich in cultural heritage, bamboo finds applications in construction, textiles, and more.

Bamboo In India

India boasts around 136 bamboo species, culturally significant and economically important. Distributed across states, particularly in the northeast, bamboo holds a crucial place in traditional practices, providing raw materials for various industries.
